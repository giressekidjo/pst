About Peoplestaff Technologies

A minority-owned technology firm on a mission to bridge the talent gap and accelerate digital transformation.

Our Story

Founded on Purpose,
Built for Impact

Peoplestaff Technologies was founded in 2018 by David Tiaha with a clear vision: to democratize access to top-tier IT talent and cloud expertise for organizations of all sizes — from commercial enterprises to federal agencies.

Headquartered in Germantown, Maryland, we have grown into a trusted partner for clients across the nation, operating branches in 7 states and holding a GSA Multiple Award Schedule (MAS) contract that enables streamlined procurement for government clients.

As a certified minority-owned small business, we are committed to bringing diversity, innovation, and excellence to every engagement.
